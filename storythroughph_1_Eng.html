PREFACE 2:- Why Lesbian Love ?

Lesbian love exists in our Indian Hindu culture. The celebration of the female form abounds in traditional Hindu temples. On the walls, in every corner, in every bracket, there are beautiful women. Some holding the branch of a tree (salabhanijka). Some indolently stretching themselves (alasakanya). Some dancing (nartaki). Some making music (sangitavadini). Some looking into the mirror (darpanasundari). Some fondling their own breasts (svasthanasparsha). Some exposing themselves (vasanabhramsa). Some with babies (putravallabha). Some with swords (khadgadhari). Some meditating (tapasvini). More than all, our hindu gods explored the essence of homosexuality at some point of time based on their circumstances.

An act of love making between women need not be an expression of sexual desire. A woman could desire a man but use another woman’s body as a substitute, an idea described in descriptions of Ravana’s harem where women kiss the lips of women who have been kissed by Ravana. There are images of single women, exposing their genitals to the observer or playing with their own breasts. A woman touching another woman, embracing her, adorning her, can be described as just a friend, a servant, a sister, but still these happens because they always love the touch nd feel between them. In the Vedas, the fire-god Agni has two mothers (dvi-matri) in which literally or metaphorically, it represent the two sticks used to spark off a fire. As per the myth, Lord shiva and his wife parvathi has been cursed that they never have a chance to give birth to a child. Lord ganesha is kinda a prototype formed with mud by parvathi. Karthikeyan have six karthikai sisters as his mothers who taught everything to karthikeya and has been given to shiva and parvathi.

Lord Ayyapan have an extremely different story for his birth. Mohini avataram had been created by lord Vishnu to destroy the monster BHASMASOORAM who tries to kill lord shiva. Bhasmasooran works for lord shiva. He collects the ashes from graves and give it to shiva. Lord shiva blessed him with few powers as an appreciation of his works, but that turned out badly. So Vishnu took a step as mohini avataram and destroy the monster labour. Lord shiva fell in love by seeing the beauty of this avatar and there we got a new born god Ayyapan. This story “Agni Pravesam” brings down the characters discussed above in to the reality. Agni pravesam is the story originally created with the characters from what I have heard in our epics from childhood. This is not just a story of love between two girls, but this is the story of how AGNI came to this world.

Divi has been dragged to death by wolves in front of his love who is also bleeding to death. And this is how Agni pravesam begins.
